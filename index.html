Sofia's White Chocolate Matcha Cookies Recipe

Contents:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Directions
Ingredients Quantity
Matcha powder 1 tablespoon
Butter 12 tablespoons
White sugar ¾ cup
Brown sugar ½ cup
Eggs 2 eggs
Vanilla extract 1 ½ teaspoons
All-purpose flour 2 ¾ eggs
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Baking powder ½ teaspoon
Salt ½ teaspoon
White chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Step 2: Melt 12 tablespoons butter in a microwave-safe large bowl.
Step 3: Whisk in ¾ cup granulated white sugar and ½ cup packed brown sugar.
Step 4: Whisk in 2 eggs and 1 ½ teaspoon vanilla extract.
Step 5: In a separate medium bowl, whisk together 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon matcha powder, 1 teaspoon baking soda, ½ teaspoon baking powder, and ½ teaspoon salt.
Step 6: Stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
Step 7: Stir in 2 cups white chocolate chips.
Step 8: Use a 1 ½ tablespoon cookie scoop to scoop out cookie dough, then roll into balls. Place cookie dough balls on parchment-covered sheet pan.
Step 9: Bake for 10-12 minutes, until just starting to brown around the edges.
Step 9: Let sit on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then remove and let cool on a cooling rack.
